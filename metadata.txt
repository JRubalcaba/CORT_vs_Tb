BC_CV	   - Basal glucocorticoid levels (coefficient of variation) (Vitousek et al. 2018 Sci Data)
Tb	   - Field body temperature (ºC) (Meiri 2018 Glob Ecol Biogeogr)

active	    - Estimated activity window (proportion 0-1) 
meanTe_sun	    - Mean operative temperature in the sun (ºC)

Te5	          - Minimum operative temperaure (ºC) - 5% percentile
Te95	          - Maximum operative temperature (ºC) - 95% precentile

foraging.mode   - Foraging mode (active vs sit-and-wait) (Meiri 2018 Glob Ecol Biogeogr)
Sex	          - Sex of the individual for hormone sampling (Vitousek et al. 2018 Sci Data)
